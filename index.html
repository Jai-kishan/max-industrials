OUR SERVICES

International Procurement & Supply

With the four procurement offices world worldwide, every order's progress is closely monitored to ensure prompt delivery. Prior to shipment, each order is checked for quality and quantity. The order is then packed and labelled in accordance with the directions on the purchase order.

We provide our clients with creative, all-encompassing, and economical procurement solutions. Consult with our team for:

  • Savings and reasonable prices with ongoing process enhancements
  • Reliable products & services
  • Shorter acquisition cycle times

Steel Fabrication & Installation

We can offer a broad range of steel detailing services in structural and other types of steel as full-service steel fabricators situated in Guyana South America.

We can offer our clients the following thanks to our cutting-edge 3D technology:

  • Detailed blueprints for each component
  • Comprehensive erection plans
  • Clear shop drawings
  • RFI templates in 3D
  • Full-scale templates for complicated layouts
  • Isometric details of complicated connections

Heavy Equipment Supply

Industrial Supply International Inc. offers helpful marketing and customer services for premium heavy equipment and construction supplies. We offer equipment from well-known manufacturers from all around the world.
