GET TO KNOW MAX INDUSTRIALS

A locally owned company based in Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea. Max Industrials offers unique and cost-effective propositions with achievable lead times, to help manage the logistics of tight supply chains while also catering to the standards and requirements of our clientele.

Max Industrials meets customer needs by providing high-quality, low-cost industrial equipment, factory machinery, spare parts, and solutions for all industrial, manufacturing, storage, and safety requirements. We run our company with integrity and the highest standards, with a focus on customer service. We help customers save time and money by providing them with the products they need to keep their facilities running. Our product line includes everything from small packaging items to large, heavy-duty industrial material handling equipment.

Our skilled personnel provide services using a comprehensive combination of technology and our experienced team to provide clients with an unbeatable procurement and supply chain experience. With four offices globally, we make certain that our clients' fundamental capabilities are enhanced while their supply-chain efficiency is improved. By supporting their procurement supply chains, Max Industrials provides services to a variety of industries in Papua New Guinea, including the oil and gas, mining, construction, water supply, and many more.
